WELCOME TO OUR CONFERENCE.

The 1st Advanced Computer Science Technologies (CSTG 2024) is a conference that brings together professionals from academia and industry to exchange knowledge and ideas about the latest trends and developments in computer science. The conference will feature a wide range of topics, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, and more. Join us in Beijing, China, from March 15-17, 2024, to learn from leading experts in the field and network with your peers. The venue for the conference is the Peking University, one of the most prestigious universities in China. We look forward to seeing you there!

PEKING UNIVERSITY

Peking University is a major research university located in Beijing, China. It is one of the most prestigious universities in the world and is known for its strong emphasis on research and innovation. The university has a long history of academic excellence and has produced many of China's top leaders in various fields. The campus is located in the Haidian District of Beijing and is easily accessible by public transportation. We are excited to host CSTG 2024 at this beautiful and historic campus.
