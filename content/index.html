CSTG 2026

The 4th International Conference on Computer Science and Technology (CSTG 2026) will be held in Jihadara, Planet Bian during April 3-5, 2026. CSTG 2026 is organized by the Computer Science and Technology Committee, and co-organized by the Computer Science and Technology Society. The conference is an international forum for the presentation of technological advances and research results in the fields of Computer Science and Technology. The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world.

We warmly welcome prospective authors to submit research papers to CSTG 2026, and share the valuable experiences with the scientists and scholars around the world. For details about paper submission, please refer to the Call For Papers section.

Venue

Jihadara is a city located on Planet Bian, which is the third planet in the Solar System. The city is known for its beautiful scenery and pleasant climate. Jihadara is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from all over the world. The city is also home to a number of research institutions and universities, making it an ideal location for the conference.
