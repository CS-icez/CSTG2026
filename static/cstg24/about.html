ABOUT CSTG 2024

Advanced Computer Science Technologies, CSTG for short, is a conference that brings together professionals from the computer science field. The conference is a great opportunity for professionals to share their knowledge and experience, and to learn from each other. To provide a platform for professionals to share their knowledge and experience, the conference features a variety of sessions, including keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops.

This year we choose Beijing, China as the host city for the conference. Beijing is a city with a rich history and a vibrant culture. It is also a city with a strong technology industry. We believe that Beijing is the perfect place for professionals to gather and share their knowledge and experience.

We are lokking forward to your participation in CSTG 2024, for your papers, your presentations, and your discussions. We believe that your participation will make the conference a great success.


CSTG Committee, 2024
