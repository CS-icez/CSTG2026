CSTG 2026 Call for Papers

The 4th International Conference on Computer Science and Technology (CSTG '26) will be held on April 3-5, 2026, in Jihadara, Planet Bian, sponsored by Northeast Dharma Lineage.

Important Dates

Spring deadline:

  • Paper titles and abstracts due: Tuesday, April 30, 2024, 11:59 pm UTC
  • Full paper submissions due: Tuesday, May 7, 2024, 11:59 pm UTC
  • Notification to authors: Wednesday, July 24, 2024
  • Final paper files due: Tuesday, October 15, 2024

Fall deadline:

  • Paper titles and abstracts due: Thursday, September 12, 2024, 11:59 pm UTC
  • Full paper submissions due: Thursday, September 19, 2024, 11:59 pm UTC
  • Notification to authors: Tuesday, December 10, 2024
  • Final paper files due: Thursday, March 6, 2025

Overview

CSTG aims to advance cutting-edge research and innovation in the field of computer science and technology. Any relevant topics are welcome, including systems, theory, AI, interdisciplinary areas, and more.

CSTG provides a high-quality online forum for cutting-edge research, facilitating meaningful discussions, and advancing the boundaries of technology and innovation in the field.

Two Deadlines

CSTG '26 offers authors the choice of two submission deadlines. Any paper submitted to one of these deadlines and accepted during the subsequent reviewing period will be presented at the conference and will appear as part of the proceedings. In the meantime, authors are permitted to advertise their papers as accepted by CSTG, for example, listing them on CVs. Papers rejected from one of these deadlines cannot be submitted to the immediate next deadline.

Submission Requirements

All submissions to the Computer Science and Technology Conference undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process. Authors are therefore required to anonymize their submissions and remove any identifying information, such as author names and affiliations, from the manuscript and metadata to ensure a fair and unbiased review. Submissions should not exceed 14 pages in length, including all figures, tables, and references. Papers must be formatted according to the conference template, which is available on the conference website. Submissions that do not adhere to these guidelines will be rejected without review.
