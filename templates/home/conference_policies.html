Conference Policies

Code of Conduct

All participants, including attendees, speakers, sponsors, and volunteers, are required to adhere to the following code of conduct. We are committed to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, or religion.

Harassment of conference participants in any form will not be tolerated. This includes offensive verbal comments, deliberate intimidation, stalking, following, harassing photography or recording, sustained disruption of talks or other events, inappropriate physical contact, and unwelcome sexual attention.

Submission Guidelines

All submissions must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. The following paper categories are welcome:

  • Full papers: These should present completed research results or case studies.
  • Short papers: These should present work in progress or novel ideas.
  • Posters: These should summarize research at a stage where feedback can be beneficial.

Submissions must follow the formatting guidelines provided on the conference website and be within the specified page limits.

Review Process

All submitted papers will undergo a rigorous peer-review process. Each submission will be reviewed by at least three independent reviewers. The review process is double-blind, meaning both the authors' and reviewers' identities will be kept confidential.

Reviewers will evaluate submissions based on their originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of presentation. Authors will receive feedback and may be required to make revisions before final acceptance.

Registration Policy

All attendees must register for the conference. Early bird registration discounts are available until the specified date. Registration fees cover access to all sessions, conference materials, coffee breaks, and lunches. Additional fees may apply for workshops, tutorials, and social events.

Cancellations received before the specified date will receive a full refund minus a processing fee. No refunds will be provided for cancellations received after this date.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Any personal information collected during the registration process will be used solely for the purposes of conference administration and communication. We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the conference policies, please contact us at cstg2026@gmail.com.
